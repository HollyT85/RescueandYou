Success Stories

Mischief

At 4 month old, Mischief was rescued from the pound. He had been a street dog; he was underweight and malnourished. Mischief had served his 7 days in kennels and was due to be put to sleep. Luckily, a husky rescue stepped up.
Mischief was placed in a foster home where it was soon discovered he had serious infections in all of his organs. It was thought he wouldn't survive the night.
Thanks to dilligent vets and a commited foster family, Mischief made it through and was then adopted by his fosterers; they couldn't let him go after all he had been through at such a young age... Even though he does very much live up to his name!

Little Dave

Little Dave was a pet shop reject. Nobody wanted him because he was small and had a wonky head so he was put up for rescue adoption.
Dave was adopted by a family who immediately took him to receive vet care. After extensive examinations, which cost hundreds of pounds, it was discovered that he had an ear infection; as it had not been treated, the infection had spread to his bones and the left side of his face was paralysed.
With the correct,on-going vet treatment, Little Dave lived happily for a further 6 months. His favourite things were green although he was also partial to fingers and clothes as well!

Blue

Blue was found abandoned in a field in torrential rain. He was stuck in the mud; his fur was filthy, his tail and mane were matted.
The rescue desperately sought Blue's owners but none came forward; they probably knew they would face prosecution for the state he was in.
After months of hard work, Blue is now clean, healthy and enjoying all the home comforts any horse could possibly want.
